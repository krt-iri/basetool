# IRIDIUM Basetool — CLA Signatures

This file is the public roster of Contributors who have signed the
[IRIDIUM Basetool Individual Contributor License Agreement](../CLA.md).

If You are signing for the first time, follow the procedure in
[§ 11 of the CLA](../CLA.md#11-how-to-sign): open a pull request titled
`cla: sign — <your-github-handle>` that appends a row to the table
below, and include the verbatim acceptance sentence from § 11 in the
pull-request description.

The table is intentionally minimal — Your full legal name, Your GitHub
handle, the email You sign Git commits with, the date You signed in
ISO-8601 format, and the CLA version You signed. Do not include
addresses, phone numbers, or any other personal data.
